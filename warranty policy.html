SAFE DRIVE AWAY INSTRUCTIONS

(For the first 24 hours form the time of delivery of the vehicle.)

  • Keep a window slightly open while closing doors.
  • Avoid any kind of impact to the newly fixed glass and the vehicle.
  • Restrict driving the vehicle to the minimum possible. If possible, do not drive.
  • Drive smoothly, Avoid driving on rough road
  • Do not wash car.
  • Do not drive in rains.
